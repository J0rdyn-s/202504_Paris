📍 Statue of Liberty (La Statue de la Liberté)

A smaller version of the New York statue, installed for the 1889 World’s Fair in Paris.

📍 Eiffel Tower (La Tour Eiffel)

The most iconic symbol of Paris, built for the 1889 Exposition Universelle.

📍 Les Invalides

Home to Napoleon’s tomb and a major military museum complex.

📍 National Assembly (L’Assemblée Nationale)

The French Parliament’s lower house, located near the Seine.

📍 Musée d'Orsay

A former train station converted into a world-class museum of Impressionist art.

📍 Institut de France

The prestigious institution housing the Académie Française.

📍 Conciergerie

A former royal palace and prison where Marie Antoinette was held before her execution.

📍 Notre-Dame Cathedral

A Gothic masterpiece located on the Île de la Cité, overlooking the Seine.
